How does it work?

PEAR stands for Peer Evaluation Autonomus Reputation Journal. The goal of this project is to allow the scientific community to decide on the merit of contributions and encourage honest participation in the peer-review process without relying on traditional journals.

The result is a fully autonomous community of reviewers and authors whose participation is rewarded by the blockchain in Reputation. Reputation is gained by making valuable contributions to the community in the form of high quality papers and unbiased honest reviews.

  • Trusted Oracle-based distribution of Reputation by smart contract
  • Staking of reputation to ensure scalability and quality of content
  • Double-blind peer review process
  • Greatly reduced publication cost
